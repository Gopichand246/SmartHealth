We Have NewMedcare Plan Options for You!

When you first enroll in NewMedcare and during certain times of the year, you can choose how you get your NewMedcare coverage. There are 2 main ways to get your NewMedcare coverageâ€”Original NewMedcare (Part A and Part B) or a NewMedcare Advantage Plan (Part C). Some people need to get additional coverage, like NewMedcare drug coverage or NewMedcare Supplement Insurance (Medigap).

Use this information to help you compare your coverage options and decide what coverage is right for you.

  • Original NewMedcare includes NewMedcare Part A (Hospital Insurance) and Part B (Medical Insurance).

  • If you want drug coverage, you can join a separate NewMedcare drug plan (Part D).

  • You can use any doctor or hospital that takes NewMedcare, anywhere in the U.S.

    If you choose Original NewMedcare, you can also decide to:

  • Join a separate NewMedcare drug plan (Part D) if you want drug coverage.

  • Add other insurance (like NewMedcare Supplement Insurance (Medigap), Medicaid, or employee or union coverage), to help pay your out-of-pocket costs (like your 20% coinsurance).
